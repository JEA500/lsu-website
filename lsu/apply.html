Programme Overview

Bachelor of Laws (LLB) at UPSA Law School

The UPSA Law School invites applications for admission to its Bachelor of Laws (LLB) Degree programme for the 2025/2026 Academic Year. The LLB Degree programme is offered in two cohorts:

  • Three (3)-Year LLB Degree Programme for First Degree Holders: An evening school programme designed for students with a first degree in any discipline who wish to pursue a career in law.
  • Four (4)-Year LLB Degree Programme for Non-First Degree Holders: A full-time day programme for students with secondary school qualifications.

The programme is rigorous and prepares students for admission into professional training at the Ghana School of Law.
