About Us

Empowering Future Legal Professionals

Law Student Union is dedicated to representing and supporting law students at every stage of their academic journey.

We advocate for student needs, provide academic resources, and host professional development programs, including career workshops. Our initiatives also promote mental health, and general student well-being. Through social events, competitions, and community-building activities, we create a vibrant and inclusive environment where all students can thrive.

We work to enhance the law school experience and uphold the interests of all students.

Academic Support

(Makola Aid Initiative and Resit Success Initiative)

The Makola Aid Initiative aims to prepare UPSA law students for the Makola entrance exam. The alumni serve as tutors who help in preparing the students for the Makola entrance exam. This program will be completely free, as it is designed to be highly effective to help increase the success rate for entry into Makola.

The Resit Success initiative is designed to support students who are retaking a course. There will be peer-to-peer tutoring sessions carefully scheduled to help a resit student find their feet. Along with academic support, emotional support will be offered through the UPSA Counseling Unit to help resit students stay motivated and manage stress during their journey. All information will remain confidential for those enrolled in the program as the means of signing up is through a Google form.

Juris & Jots

Juris & Jots is designed to equip students with essential writing skills while blending legal precision with creative expression. Writing is at the core of legal education and practice, and this initiative will provide students with structured training in legal research, advocacy writing, and opinion drafting in collaboration with publication platforms such as the Law Platform and Dennislaw coupled with the participation in both internal and international legal writing competitions.

Prof Abotsi Legal Bank

This is a premier digital resource designed to support students, legal practitioners, and academics with a rich collection of carefully curated reading materials and landmark legal cases. Built with the vision of making legal research more accessible and efficient, our platform brings together essential texts, case law, and commentary across various areas of law.
